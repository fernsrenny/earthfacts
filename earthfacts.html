A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the largest of the terrestrial planets. It is the only known planet to support life and was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago. Unlike other planets, Earth is not named after a Greek or Roman deity.

About the Earth

  • Polar Diameter: 12,714 km
  • Mass: 5.97 × 1024 kg
  • Moons: 1 (The Moon)

Top 3 Facts about the Earth

  1. The Earth’s rotation is gradually slowing.
  2. It was once believed that Earth was the center of the universe.
  3. Earth has a powerful magnetic field.
